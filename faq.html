FAQ & Rules

Welcome to the central hub for all questions, rules, and important info about New Worlds Order. Here you’ll find how the region operates, how members interact, and the key policies that keep our community running smoothly.

Important:

All rules, regulations, and content on this website can only be modified through a majority poll vote in the region. Changes without a vote are invalid.

Rules of Conduct

  • Respect other nations — debates are encouraged, personal attacks are forbidden.
  • Keep roleplay immersive, fun, and engaging.
  • Obey the structure of the government; hierarchy exists for organization.
  • No spamming or abusing the RMB or regional communication channels.
  • Harassment, griefing, or attempts to destabilize the region will result in removal.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I join the government? → Contribute actively, stay involved, and you may be noticed for promotion.
  • Do I have to roleplay? → Roleplay is optional but encouraged for a richer experience.
  • Can I form alliances with other nations? → Yes, but consult leadership first.
  • How do I move up ranks? → Active participation, loyalty, and consistent contributions.
  • What happens if I break the rules? → Violations are warned first; repeat offenses may lead to removal.
