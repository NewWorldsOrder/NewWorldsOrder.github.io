Government of the NWO

The New Worlds Order is built on a balance of power and structure. Our leadership is defined not only by the official command ranks but also by a unique system of aesthetic titles that grant influence and prestige without direct control. Together, these two systems shape how the region is governed.

Command Structure

The primary government of the New Worlds Order is organized into distinct power positions, each with a clear purpose:

  • [Supreme Court] — Head of State and ultimate authority of the Order.
  • [High Court] — Deputy leader and second-in-command.
  • [The Tribunal] — Oversees military law, succession, and internal discipline.
  • [The Judiciary] — Manages diplomacy, propaganda, and external affairs.
  • [BEG] — Guards our borders and controls regional admission.
  • [Peacekeeper] — Maintains embassies and foreign relations.
  • [Voice General] — Directs official announcements and communication.
  • [Data Hunter] — Gathers intelligence, runs polls, and supports decision-making.

Every office is bound by loyalty to the Constitution, and each can be removed for treason, inactivity, or misconduct.

Aesthetic Ranks

Alongside the command structure exists a system of ceremonial titles. These ranks grant no technical authority, but they carry cultural weight and a voice of influence in the region. They are marks of respect, loyalty, and contribution.

  • Finalis Auctoritatis — The Final Authority
    Permanent title of Cratens and Big Cuba. The highest symbolic rank.
  • Electi Insignes — The Distinguished Ones
  • Princeps Circulus — The First Circle
  • Consilium Sapientium — The Council of the Wise
  • Discipuli Ordinis — The Apprentices of the Order
  • Comitia Publica — The Public Assembly
  • Peregrini Novi — The Newcomers

How It Works

  • Peregrini Novi have no say in governance but may take part in basic discussions and roleplay.
  • Comitia Publica elect the Discipuli Ordinis, ensuring the people’s voice is represented.
  • Discipuli Ordinis oversee community activity, represent the Public Assembly, and submit proposals to the Council.
  • Consilium Sapientium handle disputes, review legislation, and act as senior advisors.
  • Princeps Circulus stand as guardians of the founding vision and decide promotions into higher ceremonial ranks.
  • Finalis Auctoritatis remain permanent symbolic leaders, untouched at the top of the hierarchy.
